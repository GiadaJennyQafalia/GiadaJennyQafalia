## About Me

During my Psychology degree, I had a realisation: most of 
psychology is science — and science is measurement. The only 
way to truly understand human behaviour is to observe it, 
quantify it, and let the data reveal what the naked eye can't.

That's what drew me to psychometrics first, and then to data 
analysis. A Data & AI Week with Develhope introduced me to 
machine learning and AI, and showed me how much further 
these tools could take that same curiosity.

Now I'm building my skills in Python, SQL and data analysis, 
with a focus on behavioural and people-centred contexts — 
HR analytics, healthcare, and research. I'm actively looking 
for a junior role where I can grow, contribute, and keep 
learning from real-world data challenges.
